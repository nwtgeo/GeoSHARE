APP library

Behold - the end result of our geo-spatial wizardry: the applications to deliver geographic content to the end users via web interfaces and simple, intuitive application interfaces.

Click on the bold title or thumbnail below to access the applications.





SDW: Spatial Data Warehouse

The catch-all, legacy Geocortex application that integrates and consumes the entire slew of our ISO themed WMS (web mapping services).










ATLAS

Lands Administrations mapping application displaying parcels and leases throughout the landbase of the Northwest Territories.










MTV: Mineral Tenure Viewer

Mining Recorders Office go-to app for displaying current data on mineral leases, claims, sketches, permits and much more ground extraction excitement.










ILC: Inventory of Landscape Change

ENR's one stop spatial shop for displaying human and natural disturbances on the landbase throughout NWT.

StoryMaps

Story maps are a fantastic platform upon which you can share geo content in a rich, immersive, simple yet intuitive way over the interwebs.
Story maps are easy to make and allow you to integrate a variety of different mediums (maps, photos, videos, URLs, etc.) into a slick UI/UX that can really share a message and visually drive home an idea.

This is a powerful tool in the geo toolbox.
